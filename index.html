Welcome to Tiger's Tone, where the art of music and the spirit of the wild unite! Born out of a passion for both craftsmanship and the untamed beauty of nature, Tiger's Tone was founded with a vision to create guitars that roar with exceptional sound and embody the strength of the jungle.

Our journey began in 1998, and since then, we have been crafting extraordinary instruments that cater to the diverse needs of musicians across the globe. Each Tiger's Tone guitar is an exquisite blend of precision engineering and artistic expression, meticulously handcrafted by our team of dedicated artisans.
